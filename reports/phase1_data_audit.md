## Duplicate `(term, docket)` resolution

Investigated at Stop B: the duplicate is **Medellin v. Texas, term=2007, docket=06-984**. SCDB has two `caseId`s for the same docket: `2007-026` (merits decision, 3/25/2008, 6-3) and `2007-073` (per-curiam stay denial, 8/5/2008, 5-4). The Oyez oral argument transcript belongs to the original merits case. `build_dataset.py` dedupes SCDB on `(term, docket, justice)` keeping the earliest `dateDecision`, so each Justice's text is paired with the merits vote (`2007-026`) and the per-curiam stay denial vote is dropped.
